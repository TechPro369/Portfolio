The EIT credential is earned by passing the Fundamentals of Engineering (FE) exam, the first milestone toward Professional Engineer (PE) licensure. It validates mastery of core engineering principles including structural analysis, transportation, geotechnical engineering, and mathematics — essential for practicing civil engineers in municipal infrastructure roles.
